Third Prototype

Improved the game concept



Instead of enemies emerging on the edge and head directly to the mother plant, they will spawn from designated places and follow the path to the tree. Player can place diffensive turrets on certain blocks to attack the enemies on the path.


There are three main types of enemies: Caterpillar, Butterfly, and Wasp.
Each of them has different speed/attack points/HP.
Bosses are of the same creatures, but with much more HP and much slower speed.
They are spawned from two spawner in a time period called wave, and there are total of ten waves
with different numbers of enemies.
