GAME

Game Discription

We are currently working on multiplayer tower-defence game that involved rousource management and fun interation among players.

One day, aliens came to the earth and destroy the entire vegetation except for the giant mother plant.
Soon after, brave soldiers with specialized skills binded up and stood up for defending the aliens, in hope they can someday restore their plants.
Only hope is to kill aliens, take back their resources, and then heal the mother plant that also gives powers to the soldiers.
You, as a player, have to interact and colaborate with your team mate to kill all the aliens so that the mother plant can florish once again.

Details

Rules

Destroy the enemies before they kill the mother plant.
Each time you kill an alien, they drop resources.
Resources include:
Water
Nutrition
Sunlight
Use the resources to heal the mother plant.
As the mother plant heals, it increases all the player's skill points.

Obstackes

Enemies are repeatedly generated and attack the mother plant.
After a certain amount of enemies are generated in a certain time duration called wave, they stop being created.
Players can then heal the plant and get ready for the next wave.
The consecutive wave will contain enemies that have stronger attack point.
